THE ROUTE

Four days along one of North America's most beautiful coastlines.

Beginning in Bellingham, the route crosses the Strait of Georgia, follows Vancouver Island's eastern shoreline, winds through old-growth forests to the Pacific, and finishes with one final evening in Vancouver.

DAY ONE

Crossing Into Island Time

≈ 76 driving miles 1 ferry crossing

Leave Bellingham early enough to enjoy the ferry crossing instead of rushing through it. Once on Vancouver Island, settle into Tigh-Na-Mara Resort and let the pace slow down.

Walk Rathtrevor Beach at low tide, enjoy dinner overlooking the Strait of Georgia, and prepare for tomorrow's drive toward the Pacific Rim.

  • Tsawwassen → Duke Point ferry
  • Tigh-Na-Mara Seaside Spa Resort

THE ROAD COMPANION

The Lucid Charging Plan

The 2023 Lucid Air Touring leaves Bellingham at 100%. A deliberate fast-charge in Port Alberni protects the west-coast leg, followed by Electrify Canada's 350 kW Nanaimo station on the return. No charging at Pluvio is assumed.

350 KW WHEN POSSIBLE

Charge less. Keep moving.

The plan uses the conservative 384-mile Touring configuration and keeps at least a 15% arrival reserve as the target. Live navigation and charger status always take priority over these estimates.

DAY THREE · OVERNIGHT

Loden Hotel · Vancouver

1177 Melville Street

~7 kW assumed · confirm charger power with valet 50–60% estimated arrival SoC 80–90% target SoC after charging 3–6 hr estimated charging time

Ask valet to charge overnight. No Day 4 fast-charging stop should be needed for the drive back to Bellingham.

BACKUP · NON-PREFERRED NETWORK

BC Hydro Rock City Centre

2980 Island Highway, Nanaimo · up to 400 kW

Use if Electrify Canada is unavailable or congested on Day 3. If the Port Alberni hub is unavailable on Day 2, use BC Hydro's two 50 kW chargers at 3455 Johnston Road before heading west.

Route to each station in the Lucid to precondition the battery. Check live status before leaving Parksville and Ucluelet; weather, elevation, speed, wheel choice, battery temperature, charger sharing, and charging taper can change actual range and session time. Estimates use the Touring's 92 kWh battery and exclude queuing and charger setup time.
